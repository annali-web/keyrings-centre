FAQ

Got questions? See our answers or contact us on 1300 887 960

At Keyrings Centre, we make the process easy so you can sit back and relax. From placement of your order to delivery to your door, we take care of it all. What's the minimum order?

The minimum order is 20 keyrings. And don't forget the more your order, the bigger the discount. Contact us for further details on bulk discounts.

How should I supply my artwork?

Files can be supplied in the following formats in either CMYK or PMS colours.

  • PDF - Preferred format
  • EPS and AI - please ensure fonts are created in outlines
  • TIFF - at 300dpi

Or we can set your artwork for you at a small fee.

How are my keyrings packaged?

Your keyrings will be supplied separately in poly bags. Customised packaging is also available, such as velvet pouches and branded boxes. Contact our friendly customer service team for a quote on customised packaging.

When will I get my order?

Samples will arrive within 7-10 days so you can see what your keyring will look like. Your completed order will arrive within 15-21 days depending on the size of the order and complexity of the design.

Where do you deliver to?

We deliver all over Australia using Australian Freight, or for worldwide deliveries, we use DHL Air Freight direct to your door.

For large orders, we use Sea Freight to save you money.

How do I pay?

You can pay using credit cards, Paypal or direct bank transfer.
